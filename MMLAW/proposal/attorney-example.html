Experience & Practice

John M. Murray is a founding partner of Murray Morin & Herman, P.A., with over 25 years of experience in complex civil litigation, aviation law, and insurance defense. He represents major corporations, insurance carriers, and aviation entities in high-stakes litigation throughout Florida and federal courts.

Mr. Murray's practice focuses on aviation accidents, products liability, construction defects, insurance coverage disputes, and appellate advocacy. He has successfully defended clients in multi-million dollar cases involving catastrophic injuries and has argued before state and federal appellate courts, including the Florida Supreme Court and the Eleventh Circuit Court of Appeals.
