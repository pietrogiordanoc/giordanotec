Appellate Practice

Full Service Appellate Representation in Florida State and Federal Courts

Experienced Appellate Advocacy

Murray Morin & Herman, P.A. has a full service appellate practice group whose attorneys are licensed to practice in all Florida state and federal courts of appeal including the Florida Supreme Court, the United States Supreme Court, the Eleventh Circuit Court of Appeals, and the United States Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit.

Our appellate attorneys regularly handle appeals involving products liability, insurance coverage, insurance bad faith, the Florida Sunshine in Litigation Act, premises liability, statute of limitations, educational malpractice, injunctions, and contract claims.
