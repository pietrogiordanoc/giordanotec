The Problem

Your website runs on WordPress—a platform that was revolutionary 15 years ago but has become slow and maintenance-heavy in 2026.

What this means practically: Your site loads in 3-4 seconds. Google penalizes sites that take longer than 2 seconds, which hurts your search rankings. Visitors on mobile (where most traffic comes from) experience even slower loads.

Additionally, WordPress requires constant updates and plugin maintenance. Every update carries risk of something breaking. Law firms are also frequent targets for WordPress hacks, requiring vigilant security monitoring.

The modern alternative: Platforms like React/Next.js (used by major corporations) are faster, more secure, and require almost zero ongoing maintenance. Your site would load in under 1 second, and you'd never worry about plugin conflicts again.
