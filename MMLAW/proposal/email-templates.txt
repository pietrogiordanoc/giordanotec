// MEETING SCRIPT (si logran agendar)
// ============================================

INTRO (30 seconds):
"Thanks for taking the time. I'll be direct: I found several critical vulnerabilities on your site that are costing you cases. I'm here to show you what they are and how we fix them."

DISCOVERY (2 minutes):
- "Walk me through your current process: How do most clients find you?"
- "What percentage come through the website vs. referrals?"
- "Have you noticed a decline in web leads in the past year?"

DIAGNOSIS (5 minutes):
- Screen share the landing page
- Walk through each problem visually
- Ask: "Were you aware of [specific issue]?"

SOLUTION (3 minutes):
- "Here's what I propose..." (show pricing table)
- "The Professional tier at $15K gives you everything you need."
- "Timeline is 45 days from deposit to launch."

CLOSE (2 minutes):
- "Do you need to discuss with other partners, or can we move forward today?"
- If yes: "Great, I'll send the contract by end of day."
- If no: "What concerns do you have?" (handle objections)

OBJECTION HANDLING:
- "That's expensive": "Compared to what? One lost case costs $200K+."
- "We need to think about it": "What specifically do you need to think about?"
- "Can you do it for less?": "I can, but we'd have to cut [X, Y, Z]. Is that acceptable?"
