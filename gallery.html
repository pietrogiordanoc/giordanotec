From Concept to Final Design:

Creating a Burger Flyer

Capturing the Perfect Shot

Every great design starts with a powerful image. Before opening Photoshop, we ensure the product's essence is captured through a professional photoshoot. Lighting, composition, and fine details make all the difference.

With the perfect shot ready, we move on to digital enhancement. Colors are adjusted, contrasts are improved, and the image gets that vibrant, mouthwatering look that makes the design stand out. Every pixel counts when it comes to making a product look delicious and professional.

Design & Composition

Now, creativity takes control. We choose the best image, integrate graphic elements, select the right typography, and craft an eye-catching, well-balanced layout.

Final Artwork & Application

The flyer is ready. Now, we prepare it in different formats for both digital and print distribution. It's optimized for social media, high-quality printing, and ad campaigns.
