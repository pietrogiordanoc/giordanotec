Our Positioning

GiordanoTec is the professional brand name of Pietro Giordano, an independent designer and multimedia specialist with years of field experience across branding, print, web aesthetics, and visual production.

This is not an anonymous content factory. It is a focused, high-touch creative practice for clients who need visual quality, brand consistency, and execution discipline.

We operate with boutique studio standards: senior-level craft, direct communication, and measurable attention to detail from concept to final delivery.

Core Services

  • Photography, video, and post-production
  • Creative assets for social media and email campaigns
  • Web visual design for desktop and mobile experiences

How We Work

  • Structured production workflow with revision checkpoints

  • Practical solutions designed for real business use, not vanity pieces
